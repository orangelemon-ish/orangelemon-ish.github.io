About Me

Hello! This is Avni Gupta. I'm a somewhat disheveled gremlin with far too many interests to be counted.



Passions

Of passions I have many, including but not limited to cybersecurity, being destroyed by my little brother at solving Rubik's Cubes, and eating chocolate.



Reading

I've loved to read books of all sorts since I was a kid, though my favorite genre is fantasy - I love stories with a sense of whimsy. Some of my go-tos include Six of Crows by Leigh Bardugo, Hamnet by Maggie O'Farrell, and The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue by V.E. Schwab.



Cybersecurity

My latest major interest is cybersecurity and all its aspects, from programming to computer architecture to networking. I'm especially interested by the Internet of Things.

Currently, I've been named a National Cyber Scholar by the National Cyber Scholarship Foundation, and am working towards my GFACT certification in the industry.



Gardening

I adore gardening in all varieties, from vegetable garden plots to keeping houseplants. Currently, I have 7 plants - two kinds of cacti, 3 succulents, an aloe, and a peperomia. In addition to volunteering at both the high school and the teen center's community gardens, I cultivate an annual vegetable garden in my backyard, growing tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, squash, and sometimes corn.

Left: my moon cactus, Evelyn



Neuroscience and Psychology

The inner workings of the brain fascinate me. I love learning what makes people tick, and understanding how everything from brain structure to environment can influence that.
